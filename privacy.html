Privacy Policy for Caption Architect

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Information We Collect

The app only collects text you enter into the caption generator. No personal information such as names, emails, photos, contacts, or device identifiers is collected.

How Your Data Is Used

The text you enter is sent to the Groq API to generate captions. No data is stored, saved, sold, or shared.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following services:

  • Groq API – AI caption generation
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases

In-App Purchases

All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The app does not store payment information.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 18 and older.

Data Security

The app does not store any user data.
